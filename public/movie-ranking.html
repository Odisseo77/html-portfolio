The Best Movies According to Francesco

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Once Upon A Time In America

An intense and dramatic movie from 1984 directed by Sergio Leone and starring Robert De Niro.

Compagni Di Scuola (Schoolmates)

A 1988 Italian comedy-drama directed by and starring Carlo Verdone.

Carlito's Way

A 1993 American crime drama film directed by Brian De Palma and starring Al Pacino.
